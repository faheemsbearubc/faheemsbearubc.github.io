A world of bionics

Amidst the increasing demand for Bionic devices, growing public interest in Bionics, and UBC’s strategic positioning towards the field, we have created a new Design Team, BEAR, to foster excellence in the field of Bionics. Our goal is to generate solutions to replace human physiology, either by prosthesis or implants.

Innovating Devices of Tomorrow

BEAR UBC provides students with the resources to innovate cutting edge technology, immersing them in the latest in bionics innovations and research

Giving Back


BEAR provides a platform for students to give back to the community of Canada by providing low cost Bionics, and educating people about Bionics.

Generating Leaders for Tomorrow

BEAR provides a platform for enriching and developing the future STEM Bionics leaders of the future.

APPLY

Mechanical Engineer

As a Mechanical Engineer, you will be working closely with our leads to generate the actuation system for the fingers and wrist, generate a waterproof and dustproof enclosure for the hand via the use of composites, and specify and determine the placement of sensors which provide feedback to the user on various environmental objects and their properties, including geometry and rigidity.

APPLY

Software Engineer

As a software Engineer, you will be working with our software and signal section on generating the UI and UX for our hand's companion app, working with machine learning to classify new input data, and working with integrating voice control on our companion app. Preference will be given to applicants who have shown past experience in C++, HTML, Bootstrap, and UI & UX design.

APPLY

Controls and Signals Engineer

As an control and signals engineer, you will be responsible for generating PCB schematics and functions in Python and Matlab to ensure that signals read from our various sensors can be interpreted correctly. Our Engineers are currently working on complex problems involving the use of FSR's, load cells, IR's, and capacitors.

APPLY

Outreach Team

As a member of the outreach team, you will be responsible for ensuring the security of the lifeblood of our team: sponsorship. Get the chance to network with some of the best companies in Vancouver as you promote us and learn how to manage finances, bill of materials, and our supply chain.
